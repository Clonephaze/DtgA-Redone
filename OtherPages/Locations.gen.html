Gadget Locations

Each of these locations, some old some new, hide one of DtgA's many Gadgets!

Old Man's Cabin

The old man's cabin in Hope's Rise, now hiding away the flint strike gadget.

Hopes Rise, east of the southern shrine.

Hopes Watch

Newly added Dungeon below the western shrine on Hope's Rise, hiding away the slingshot gadget (among other things).

Hopes Rise, below the western shrine.

Cannon Cave

Newly added cave, here to offer the Cannon gadget and give you your first place to test it.

Behind the Woodland stable.

Lanayru Waterfall

The waterfall in Lanayru's Promenade, hiding away the Silver Gauntlets.

Lanayru Promenade, east of Kakariko Village.

Desert Ruins

Newly added Desert Ruins in the westernmost reaches of the Gerudo desert, hiding away the Twilight Spinner.

Just south-west of the Kema Zoos shrine, or the entrance to the valley leading to the Yiga Hideout.

Hylian Waterworks

Newly added Hylian Waterworks, hiding away the Iron Boots.

The very entrance of the Hylia Lake inlet, just south of Scout's Hill.

Hylian Royal Crypt

Resting underneath the Sanidin Park Ruins, the Royal Crypt hides away the Hover Boots.

Underneath the horse fountain in the Sanidin Park Ruins.

Dueling Monastery

Newly added Dueling Monastery, hiding away the Hookshot.

Atop the Dueling Peaks, on the Southern peak.

Hebra Mansion

Newly added structure in the Hebra Mountains, hiding away the Gust Jar.

Rest's atop the northernmost part of the Hebra Headspring.

Fort Hateno Keep

The newly added Keep structure, towering over the Fort Hateno gates, hides away the Generator.

On the cliff just to the north and behind the Fort Hateno gates.
