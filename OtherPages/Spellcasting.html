Apocalypse Spell Package

Welcome to Apocalypse Spell Package, the largest module within the expansive mod of Dangerous to go Alone.

Discover your spellbook, the Grimoire Lorul, concealed within a shrine upon Hope's Rise, granting access to a repertoire of short and long-range elemental attacks. Once discovered summon your book by holding crouch↺ + pressing run⇒. You will need to summon your book whenever you wish to switch your active element type.
Wish you could switch your active element type, mid combat? Find the Cube of Zoey, lost in the expanse of Hyrule, and it will enable seamless elemental swaps without consulting the spellbook, offering true mastery over your new mystical arts. Once found you can switch your active element by holding run ⇒ + pressing dpad-down ↡.
This page will serve as your guide to mastering the Elements and Casting types, unveiling the intricacies of each to empower your journey within this mystic realm.

Items and Details

Current Element Types

Aero Air
Cryo Ice
Electro Lightning
Pyro Fire

Casting Types

Melee

  • Adds a short range elemental weapon in your weapon slots
    • Hold Dpad-Down ↡ + press Throw ↝

Ranged

  • Adds a long ranged elemental weapon in your weapon slots
    • Hold Dpad-Down ↡ + press Bow↛

Area

  • Will spawn a large damaging area of effect surrounding the caster
    • Hold Dpad-Down ↡ + press Rune ↜

Scaling your spell power

All spells in DtgA scale by leveling your attributes at the coiled sword. (Crouch ↺ + Attack ⇑)

Endurance

  • Levels up Aero

Mana

  • Levels up Cryo

Agility

  • Levels up Electro

Vitality

  • Levels up Pyro

Spellbook

The book that grants you access to all the spells in DtgA.
Summon the book by pressing (Hold crouch↺ + press run⇒) and talk to the book.

Cube of Zoey

Finding the cube of Zoey grants you access to quick change your currently equipped element by cycling through until you have your desired element, even mid combat!
Switch your active element with (holding run ⇒ + pressing dpad-down ↡)
