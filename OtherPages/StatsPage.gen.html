Level Up!

Level Up and Enhance Your Power

During your journey across Hyrule with DtgA, you will need 10 crucial stats that define the essence of the Lost One: Agility, Courage, Dexterity, Endurance, Faith, Grudge, Mana, Strength, Vitality, and Wisdom. Each of these stats holds the key to unlocking or returning different facets of your strength, shaping the way you traverse the world, confront challenges, and wield your newfound abilities.

To enhance these attributes, you must harness the ancient power of the Coiled Sword. Summon this sacred relic or visit it within the Shrine of Resurrection. Offer up your rupees and Stray Souls as catalysts for growth, channeling your wealth into raw power. As you invest in your stats, watch as your potential grows, making you a formidable force in your quest to reclaim Hyrule.

Stats

Agility

Agility is responsible for increasing movement speed, as well as improving skill with small swords. It also boosts attack speed and damage for weapons that scale with Agility. Some skills like Double Jump, Double Dodge, Dodge Roll, and Air Dash scale with Agility, increasing their distance and effectiveness.

At level 8, your sneak speed will be increased.

Summon the Coiled Sword

Courage

Courage affects Master Sword cooldown rate, improves damage with the Master Sword, and increases its attack speed. It also boosts the health of all your allies and decreases the mana cost of Flurry Rush.

At level 8, your Master Sword will instantly recover its energy.

Summon the Coiled Sword

Dexterity

Dexterity improves the damage and attack speed of all spear-type weapons and other weapons that scale with Dexterity. It also enhances the effect of some abilities and increases the speed of all dodges. If a thief expansion is added, Dexterity will be crucial for stealing, lockpicking, and pickpocketing.

At level 8, you will gain an automatic dodge when taking damage during combat.

Summon the Coiled Sword

Endurance

Endurance boosts stamina and climbing ability, increases the speed of ZGlide, and enhances your climbing, running, and sprinting speed. It also increases the stamina restored by the Flask of Hylian Flame and when resting at camp.

At level 8, your stamina will periodically restore automatically.

Summon the Coiled Sword

Faith

Faith increases Link's ability to summon allies, enhances ZGlider speed, and boosts the amount of health restored by Fierce Deity Mode. It also reduces the rupee cost of Fierce Deity Mode and decreases the amount of rupees lost on death.

At level 8, if a summon is present, there is a chance they will sacrifice their life to save the hero.

Summon the Coiled Sword

Grudge

Grudge increases Link's resistance to Blight, poison, and other status effects, while also unlocking higher-tier incantations. It also boosts the power of special spells and increases movement speed when influenced by Grudge.

At level 8, the power of the Master Sword increases when under a status abnormality.

Summon the Coiled Sword

Mana

Mana increases spellcasting ability, improves mana regeneration rate, and decreases the mana cost for spells. It also increases the speed at which spirit orbs regenerate and boosts the number of spirit orbs restored by the Flask of Hylian Tears.

At level 8, you can attune spells from multiple elements.

Summon the Coiled Sword

Strength

Strength increases the damage and attack speed of all large swords and other weapons that scale with Strength. It also improves the cooldown for ZRush, enhances the damage of charge attacks, and boosts your carrying capacity, allowing you to carry more gadgets.

At level 8, you gain the ability to automatically block damage while dodging, walking, or standing still.

Summon the Coiled Sword

Vitality

Vitality increases health and healing, boosts health restoration by the Flask of Hylian Flame, and improves health regeneration outside of combat. At higher levels, this regeneration extends to combat as well, and your resistance to damage increases.

At level 8, you will gain health regeneration outside of combat.

Summon the Coiled Sword

Wisdom

Wisdom increases the number of spells you can recall, decreases the cooldown for all spells, and unlocks higher-tier incantations. It also increases the attack speed and damage for magical weapons and all spells that scale with Wisdom. Each level of Wisdom enhances the power of your spells, giving them new effects and substantially increasing their damage.

At level 8, you gain Mana Siphon when taking damage.

Summon the Coiled Sword
