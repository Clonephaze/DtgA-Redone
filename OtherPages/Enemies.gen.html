Common Enemies

ReDead

The haunting return of a classic adversary! Undead and enduring, these foes withstand relentless assaults. Persistence is key, but victory is within reach!
Drop rupees and claw weapons upon death.

Can be found all around Hyrule.

Skulltula

Back in Hyrule with renewed menace, these eight-armed fiends are relentless. They swarm in numbers and boast formidable strength, demanding top-notch gear for a chance to conquer them. They are extremely weak to sources of fire.
Drop rupees upon death.

Can be found all around Hyrule.

Gibdo

Beware the Gibdo, ancient guardians of cursed tombs. These formidable foes endure strikes and wield dark arcane powers allowing them to warp around like the yiga. Approach with caution and arm yourself well for their relentless onslaught.

Can be found in the Hyrule Desert.

Bosses

The Hero's Shade

Once a candidate for the Hero's title, he now tests you when you first wake. You'll find him in front of the first tower, and in each of the four shrines on Hope's Rise ready for battle. Prove your Hero's title, and he'll reward you with 4 weapons of unique potential.

Can be found on Hope's Rise near the eastern shrine.

Cursed Puppets

They're like thunderblight, but with more magic. Theres 4 versions so it won't be the same fight every time, each one having a couple of spells to attack you with along with their innate speed. Will appear when the sky turns pale blue.

Can be found in the forests around Hyrule.

Argorok

Currently a WIP. You can climb on him if you have stasis. He WILL break halfway through the fight.

Found at the top of the Dueling Peaks.

Darknut

Currently a WIP. You can't kill him yet as the fight will require the megaton hammer.

Found at Castle Town Cathedral.

Galahad

Pretty straightforward, just stay close and nail your parries.

Found at Fort Hateno.

Lancelot

Currently a WIP. He'll seem like he's stuck in an animation, but stagger him out of it and then stay far away from his spear.

Found at the Shrine of Courage.

Robin

Can be a challenge, but you'll be fine if you take care of the adds and shoot well.

Found at the Heteno shooting range.

Miko and Sibyl

These are fast, hit hard, and deal a TON of aoe damage. The arena is basicaly made of fire so bring some Fireproof potions or a flamebreaker set.

Found in the Sanidin park.
