Companions

Zelda

Found initially in the cave of ressurrection, and in the Temple of Time from then on.

Vendors

The Castle Town Blacksmith

The Blacksmith is an npc that can upgrade your unbreakable weapons using the power of souls you find around Hyrule. The power of these souls will degrade until you upgrade the weapon with a boss soul, so until then you will degrade in levels around 100 hits.

Can be found around the south-east section of the Castle Town ruins.

The Phoebist

The Lamp Vendor is an npc that sells 4 unique lamps.

Can be found in the Black Market (And eventually all over hyrule)

The Alchemist Qosmo

The Potion Vendor is an npc that sells 3 new potions, 3 new elixirs, and 9 resins to enchant your weapon. (Will expand upon this information when the character is released.)

Can be found in the Black Market (And eventually all over hyrule)

Lost Souls

Frightened Soldiers

Ghosts of soldiers who cowered or fled during the calamity, who give the Lost One their soul in hopes it may help in battle against Ganon.

Can be found all around Hyrule.

Brave Knights

Ghosts of soldiers who sacrificed themselves to save others during the calamity, who give the Lost One their soul in hopes it may help in battle against Ganon.

Can be found all around Hyrule.

Royal Knights

Ghosts of distinguished knights who served under the Royal Guard, who give the Lost One their soul in hopes it may help in battle against Ganon.

Can be found all around Hyrule.

Royal Guardsmen

Ghosts of who served at the side of the royal family, who give the Lost One their soul in hopes it may help in battle against Ganon.

Can be found all around Hyrule.

Would be Champions

Ghosts of former rivals and prospective heroes, who give the Lost One their soul in hopes it may help in battle against Ganon.

Can be found all around Hyrule.
